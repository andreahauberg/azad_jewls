AS A JEWELRY DESIGNER, MY MISSION IS TO BREAK DOWN SOCIETAL NORMS OF GENDERED JEWELRY.

LIKE ARCHITECTURE, JEWELRY HAS ALWAYS BEEN A POLITICAL TOOL. USING BRANDING AND CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT, I AIM TO CHANGE THE UNDERSTANDING OF JEWELRY IN SOCIETY THROUGH MY DESIGNS.
