Sif Jakobs
The Linur collection

The collaboration with Sif Jakobs was To create sophisticated and fashionable jewellery in 8 karat gold and labgrown diamonds. The Línur collection is inspired by nordic nature and embodies minimalist luxury. The project also included store display and packaging.

Carre
Stonewall Do You Dare?

The collaboration with Carré was to create a gender-fluid collection containing various gemstones in gold & silver. The project was a synergetic process, from design thinking, brand- and market analysis, to prototyping in Rhino3D.
